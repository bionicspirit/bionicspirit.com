Updated: November 21, 2020 | comments

Contents:

  • Send email from localhost

Send email from localhost # 

brew install swaks

swaks --auth \
	--server smtp.mailgun.org \
	--au postmaster@mail.mydomain.com \
	--ap password \
  --from alex@mail.mydomain.com \
	--to user@domain.com \
	--h-Subject: "Hello" \
	--body 'World!'
